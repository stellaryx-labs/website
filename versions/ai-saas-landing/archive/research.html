Research

Research Topics

Research Topics

Photonics

Photonics is the study and application of generating, detecting, and manipulating light, with a focus on technologies such as lasers, fiber optics, and photonic devices.

Reconnaissance & Analysis

Involves the systematic examination and evaluation of aerospace cyber systems to gather intelligence and ensure the security of information and communication networks within the aerospace domain.

Electromagnetism

Electromagnetism is a branch of physics that explores the relationship between electric and magnetic fields, providing the foundation for understanding a wide range of phenomena, from simple magnets to complex electromagnetic waves.

Astrobiology

Astrobiology is the interdisciplinary scientific study of the origin, evolution, and distribution of life in the universe, exploring the possibility of extraterrestrial life beyond Earth.

Cybernetics

Cybernetics is an interdisciplinary field that explores the control and communication processes in living organisms and machines, seeking to understand and apply principles of feedback, regulation, and information processing for efficient system functioning.

Quantum Mechanics

Quantum mechanics is a fundamental branch of physics that describes the behavior of matter and energy at the smallest scales, introducing principles such as superposition, entanglement, and wave-particle duality.
